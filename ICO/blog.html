Our Online Programs For 2021

ICO Academy always follow the rules for the sake of student's good and health. So in this pandemic, we give our students something new. We provide online programs so that every student can learn from anywhere to prevent any disease or sickness. Other than that, now not only student from Jabodetabek can learn with ICO Academy but student all over Indonesia can join ICO Academy from their own home. For further details contact us.


But for student that still want to learn in our place, we also already opened offline classes with maximum capacity 10 student per class, we already give all the teachers and employee of ICO Academy knowledge about health protocol so don't worry, we're trying our best to keep our student healthy. The teachers and employees also has been vaccinated and will always be tested the temperature before entering our building. Our student also have to tested their temperature and wash their hand before entering the building. Removing mask is not allowed at all except when eating, drinking or in bathroom.
